Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Thank you for using Death Clock (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how our App collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Age
  • Gender
  • Height and weight
  • Lifestyle information (exercise, diet, sleep, etc.)
  • Health status information

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • App usage data
  • Error logs
  • Device information (model, OS version, etc.)

3. How We Use Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Calculating lifespan predictions

  • Providing customized health advice
  • Identifying and resolving technical issues

4. Information Sharing

Your personal information will not be shared with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When you provide explicit consent

We implement appropriate technical and administrative measures to protect your personal information. However, we cannot guarantee complete security over the internet.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

The lifespan predictions provided by this app are statistical estimates and do not guarantee actual lifespan. Do not use as a substitute for medical diagnosis or treatment, and always consult medical professionals for important health decisions.
